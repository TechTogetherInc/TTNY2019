About

Join 200 all-female and femme non-binary students from across the country at Queens College to uncover the magic of technology in just 36 hours.

Come to TechTogether New York to bring your ideas to life! With the help of workshops from industry experts across a variety of disciplines and 24-hour access to mentorship, you will leave this once-in-a-lifetime experienc ewith new skills, friends, and confidence.

No Coding Experience Required

There is an infinite number of opportunities to contribute to a project. From design to project management, well-rounded teams need individuals with a variety of skill-set. So if you're a new hacker, don't sweat it. Whatever your skills or skill level, there is a place for you at our hackathon.

FAQ

  • Where is it?

    The Dining Hall at Queens College, City University of New York 65-30 Kissena Blvd, Flushing, NY 11367

  • Who is coming?

    We are expecting about 250-300 hackers from across the country.

  • Who can attend?

    Anyone who defines themselves as female, women, non-binary, from high school to college students are welcomed! We definitely look forward to seeing FIRST TIME HACKERS!

  • Do I need experience?

    Absolutely not! Our hackathon is completely beginnerâ€™s friendly and we welcome those without any coding experience or background. There will be mentors, workshops, and other resources to help you during the hackathon!

  • Are there any rules?

    We want all of our participants to have a positive experience. We encourage you to read our code of conduct.

  • What should I bring?

    Laptop, phone, charger, something to sleep on (sleeping bag/bedsheet) if you want, toothbrush, and toothpaste.

  • Does it cost anything?

    No! 100% free!

  • Can we form teams?

    Absolutely! Whether you have a team ready as you come into the event or are unsure of who to work with, we will have a networking session so you can talk to other hackers to form a team of your choice. If you still need help finding a team, our organizers and volunteers will be there to help out!

  • Will there be reimbursements?

    We will do our best to reimburse our hackers for travel only, but as we get more sponsors on board, we will be able to determine and finalize how much reimbursement will be provided.

  • Will transportation be provided?

    We will do our best to provide a bus from Boston to New York City.
