  • phone Who do I call in case of an emergency?
    keyboard_arrow_down

    Please call 911 for an ambulance, any ASAP assistance of a life-threatening or serious injury. For any other serious or dangerous situations, please call the BU Police at 6173532121.

  • people How big can my team be?
    keyboard_arrow_down

    Each team must have minimum 2 members, maximum 5 members.

  • computer How many workshops can I attend?
    keyboard_arrow_down

    You can attend as many workshops and talks as you'd like! Our workshops are first come first serve due to limited room capacity. Once a workshop has filled up we will close the room door. You can view all our workshops on our Schedule here!

  • check_circle How are projects being judged?
    keyboard_arrow_down

    The projects will be judged on these 4 criteria: Presentation, Usefulness, Originality, and Difficulty. More details can be found on our Devpost page.

  • format_list_numberedHow many challenges can I apply to?
    keyboard_arrow_down

    You can apply to as many categories as your hack can qualify for!

  • timer How do I submit my project on Devpost?
    keyboard_arrow_down

    Please submit your project on the Devpost page here by 8am on Sunday. More details can be found on the Devpost page! Make sure to ask an organizer or ask on Slack if you have specific questions.

  • help What if I have a question not listed on here?
    keyboard_arrow_down

    Ask your question on the TechTogether Boston 2019 Slack, or to someone on the organizing team!
