How to Get Here

FROM THE NORTH
  1. Take I-93 South to Boston.
  2. Take the Massachusetts Avenue (Mass Ave.)/Albany Street Exit. Turn right onto Mass Ave.
  3. Travel along Mass Ave. and take a left onto Beacon Street.
  4. Take Beacon Street to Commonwealth Avenue.
  5. Bear right at the far end of Kenmore Square onto Commonwealth Avenue.
  6. Continue outbound on Commonwealth Avenue. Take a right onto Buick Street, and then a left at the end of the street.
FROM THE SOUTH
  1. Take I-93 North to Boston.
  2. Take the Massachusetts Avenue (Mass Ave.)/Albany Street Exit. Turn right onto Mass Ave.
  3. Travel along Mass Ave. and take a left onto Beacon Street.
  4. Take Beacon Street to Commonwealth Avenue.
  5. Bear right at the far end of Kenmore Square onto Commonwealth Avenue.
  6. Continue outbound on Commonwealth Avenue. Take a right onto Buick Street, and then a left at the end of the street.
FROM THE WEST
  1. Take the Massachusetts Turnpike (I-90) East. Take Exit 18 (Allston/Brighton).
  2. Bear left onto Cambridge Street. Follow to Harvard Street.
  3. Take a left at the set of lights onto Harvard Street. At the next set of lights turn left onto Brighton Avenue.
  4. Brighton Avenue will merge into Commonwealth Avenue (inbound). Continue on Commonwealth Avenue untilthe BU Bridge. Just before the bridge, take a right onto Essex Street and bear left onto Mountfort Street.
  5. Keep to the left, and take the first left turn off of Mountfort Street, which loops back to Commonwealth Avenue. Take a left at the light onto Commonwealth Avenue and continue until Buick Street.
  6. Take a right at Buick Street and then a left at the end of the street. You will see Agganis Arena on your left.
From Logan Airport
  1. Take the MBTA silver line shuttle to South Station.
  2. Transfer to the Red Line and take inbound to Park Street.
  3. Transfer to Green Line and take the 'B' Line outbound (important - take the 'B' Line toward Boston College) to Pleasant Street stop.
